Hi 👋 My name is Akash Withanaarachchi
======================================

Computer Engineer Undergraduate 
-------------------------------

I'm an enthusiastic undergraduate student pursuing Computer Engineering at the University of Peradeniya. My academic focus revolves around embedded systems and computer architecture. Passionate about delving into the intricacies of hardware-software integration, I strive to understand and create innovative solutions in the realm of embedded systems. With a keen interest in the architecture of computing systems, I aim to leverage this knowledge to design efficient and optimized systems. 💻🔌 Beyond academics, I actively engage in projects and extracurricular activities that allow me to apply my theoretical knowledge in practical scenarios. I am driven by the challenge of solving complex problems and am always eager to explore emerging technologies in the field of embedded systems. 🚀💡

* 🌍  I'm based in Sri Lanka
* ✉️  You can contact me at [waakashmuthumal@gmail.com](mailto:waakashmuthumal@gmail.com)
